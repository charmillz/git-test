Charlotte Miller: Project A¶

February 2, 2018

This dataset describes the mathematics performance of students from grades 3-8 in NYC schools. The graphs I created depict a variety of things, but keep demographic as a consistent variable as I found it to be the most interesting. I investigated how many students from the various demographics were tested according to year, and also compared the mean scale score amongst them. I could only do this where applicable, as some of the mean scale scores have a value of NaN, which forbids it from being used in graphing that requires a particular axis to be a numerical value.
